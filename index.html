How it stays current: A scheduled job (GitHub Actions) searches the market once a day, saves the result to a data file, and this page displays that file. So the numbers hold still between daily updates and change only when the market changes — clicking Reload re-reads the saved file, it does not re-guess. Method: AI-core companies that took a seed round and have since raised $100M+ within ~12 months (US-weighted). Categories, backers, and timing are model-surfaced from public reporting — verify specifics in PitchBook/Affinity before acting. Point the job at Glasswing's PitchBook connector for audited data.
